Ultimate Frisbee

Ultimate Frisbee is more than just a game – it's community, spirit, and competition.

How to Play

Ultimate is played 7 vs 7 on a field with end zones, similar to football.

  1. Basic Rules:
    • No running with the disc
    • 10-second stall count
    • Turnovers on dropped discs
  2. Scoring: Catch the disc in the opposing end zone
  3. Winning: First team to 15 wins
